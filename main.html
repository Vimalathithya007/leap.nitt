League for Environmental Awareness and Protection(LEAP)

**The Environmetal Student club of NIT Trichy**

Purpose

League for Environmental Awareness and Protection (LEAP) is a student club in NIT-Trichy aiming basically to disseminate the urgent issues prevailing in the environment and in the campus among the students of NIT-Trichy. The club was founded to create a new approach to spread the awareness and educate the students so that they develop a high level of competence in global environmental issues and to labor themselves in finding suitable solutions at the college level itself.

What we've done

Some of the recent activities of LEAP include participating in a global campaign at Trichy organized by the Indian Youth Climate Network, studying the feasibility of setting up a Bio gas plant in the campus, creating awareness about pressing environmental issues through stalls and documentary screenings. Driven by passion and enthusiasm, the club ultimately aims to make NITT an environmentally conscious and sustainable campus.

How to be Part of LEAP?

The Induction process will be conducted usually between August and October. The Induction poster will be circulated on social media platforms and will be given a couple of days to register.
If u are a nature lover or an environmental enthusiast passionate about working for Groot and his cousins and making them your besties, and protecting them, then LEAP is the right place for you. During the induction process, the candidates will have 2 PIs and will be given a Couple of tasks based on their domain opted. Task & Their passion towards the environment plays a vital role in judging the candidate
